Privacy Policy — Just a Mango

Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Just a Mango ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not directly collect your name, email address, or other personal contact details.

The App stores your game score locally on your device using on-device storage (shared_preferences). This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Data Collection

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. To provide and measure ads, Google and its partners may automatically collect and process certain information, which may include:

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system, language, network state)
  • Approximate, non-precise location derived from IP address
  • App interaction and ad interaction data

This data is collected by Google directly. We do not control and do not have access to the raw data Google collects. Google's use of this information is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here.

3. Your Advertising Choices

You can reset or limit ad personalization at any time through your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads on Android. You may reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertising there.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the ad networks used by the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Security

Score data is stored only on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Advertising data handled by Google is protected under Google's own security and privacy practices.

6. Rewards Disclaimer

Any "reward", "redeem", or currency value shown inside the App is part of in-game gameplay. It does not represent real money and does not create any obligation to pay real-world currency unless explicitly stated otherwise in a separate, clearly identified offer.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
